Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

BrainTeaser ("we", "our", "the app") is a puzzle and brain-training mobile game developed by Luke Austin. This policy explains what data is collected, why, and your rights over it.

1. Data We Collect

Data typeSourcePurpose
Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID) Google AdMob SDK Show personalised or contextual ads. Only collected on iOS if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
Device identifier App / Google AdMob Anonymous account creation (PlayFab) and ad frequency capping. Not linked to your identity.
App usage and interactions Google AdMob / Microsoft PlayFab Understand gameplay patterns, diagnose crashes, improve the app.
Account information (optional) Microsoft PlayFab Email address and display name — only if you choose to create a registered account. Used for cross-device leaderboards and cloud save.
Game scores and progress Microsoft PlayFab Global leaderboards and cloud backup of your high scores.

We do not collect your name, precise location, payment information, contacts, photos, or any sensitive personal data.

2. Third-Party SDKs

Google AdMob

BrainTeaser displays interstitial ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data to serve ads. On iOS, ad personalisation is only enabled if you tap Allow on the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

Google Privacy Policy

Microsoft PlayFab

Leaderboards, anonymous authentication, and optional cloud saves are powered by Microsoft PlayFab. PlayFab may collect device information and gameplay telemetry.

Microsoft Privacy Statement

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display ads to support free access to the game.
  • Maintain global leaderboards.
  • Back up your scores and settings so they are available on a new device (registered accounts only).
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app stability.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Anonymous gameplay data is retained for up to 12 months. If you have a registered account, your data is kept until you delete it. After deletion is requested, data is purged within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your country you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights:

  • In-app deletion: Open BrainTeaser → Profile → Delete Account.
  • Email request: LukeAustinDEV@outlook.com — we will respond within 30 days.

For more on deleting your account, see our Account Deletion page.

6. Children

BrainTeaser is rated for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: LukeAustinDEV@outlook.com
